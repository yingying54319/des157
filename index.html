About Me

Who Am I?

Hi I'm Yingying Lin I would like to introduce myself and my work. I completed a bachelor degree major in Design and mirror in Studio Art in the University of California, Davis. I am an enthusiastic, creative, and hard-working artist who study in a variety of settings- drawing, painting, printmaking as well as graphic, website, and fashion Design.

I would like to get into website design field in the near future so as a start I say hi here. There is still a lot to go and evolve but to see what I did, please visit my works in the design project page and art work page.

From 2014- 2018, while I was pursuing a bachelor degree in Davis, I also worked part-time as a website design intern. In the year of 2017, I worked with the founder of Techno Cultural Studies named Jesse Drew on an internship in Website design and Graphic Design. I worked with Jesse to launch a website in collaborated with the Vermont Multi-media Documentary Project which was about social justice in the 1970. This internship shaped my website design skill by providing me with a great opportunity to practice HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript knowledge. Especially my graphic skills in layouts, graphics, and contents design.

In March, 2018, I was employed by UC Davis Environmental Research Department to work on the redesign of the University website, titled “The Geospatial and Farming Systems Research Consortium (GFC).” This website is still perpetually under construction. As a website and graphic designer, I was responsible for the initial planning of the website, formulating the organization and layout, types of colors and images to be used. During my internship, I have had the opportunity to hone my skills with HTML5, CSS3, and JavaScript to produce web pages in an advance level. For instance, I have successfully using JavaScript to create an interactive map in the website using Geojson file with leaflet map. Additionally, in this job, I helped target specific audiences to increase website traffic and promote sponsorship for the University’s Website.

After I graduated from UC Davis, I worked serval weeks for a diamond jewelry company named Yadav on the redesign of the homepage. I successfully created layouts for this company. The temporary assignment allowed me to have extensive experience in working with Adobe Suite, such as Sketch, Photoshop, Illustrator, and InDesign. I have used these tools to successfully create web pages, banners, and logos.

My Current project is finishing updated my personal website yingying54319/web
